About TruthScan

Combating misinformation in the India-Pakistan context

Why We Built This Tool

In the India-Pakistan context, misinformation can have serious consequences, heightening tensions and potentially leading to real-world harm. TruthScan was developed to provide a reliable tool for anyone to quickly assess the credibility of news articles.

Our technology combines rule-based analysis with pattern recognition to identify characteristics commonly found in fake news. While no automated system is perfect, TruthScan offers a valuable first step in determining whether content requires additional verification.

Important Disclaimer

This tool provides an automated assessment and should not be the sole basis for determining the veracity of news. Always cross-check information with multiple reliable sources.
